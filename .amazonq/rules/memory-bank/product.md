# Product Overview

## Project Identity
**Name:** RAIL (formerly STACK)  
**Type:** Mobile-first Web3-native investment application  
**Platform:** Cross-platform iOS and Android using React Native

## Purpose & Value Proposition
RAIL is a hybrid Web3 + traditional finance investment platform designed specifically for Gen Z users who are underserved by traditional banks and overwhelmed by complex crypto tools. It bridges the trust and usability gap by enabling instant wealth-building through:

- Fiat-to-stablecoin on-ramps with seamless conversion
- Investment in stocks, ETFs, and options through traditional brokerage
- Protective AI CFO guidance for financial decision-making
- Managed wallet infrastructure that abstracts Web3 complexity

## Target Users

### Primary Persona: Taylor – The Conscious & Connected Investor
- **Age:** 22, digitally native Gen Z
- **Behavior:** Lives on TikTok, Instagram, Reddit, Discord; expects fast, engaging, intuitive experiences
- **Financial Profile:** Uses Cash App/Venmo, dabbles in Robinhood, avoids crypto due to complexity
- **Values:** Financial independence, safety, sustainability, social impact
- **Goals:** Travel fund, apartment savings, building a safety net

### Secondary Personas
- **Jordan (21):** Banking-frustrated beginner dealing with clunky traditional banking and delays
- **Chris (19):** Crypto-curious but overwhelmed by seed phrases, gas fees, and irreversible mistakes

## Key Features & Capabilities

### Core MVP Features
1. **User Onboarding & Managed Wallet**
   - Simple email/phone sign-up with passcode-based app access
   - Automatic secure wallet creation using Circle Developer-Controlled Wallets
   - No seed phrase complexity; custody fully abstracted

2. **Stablecoin Funding Flow**
   - USDC deposits from Ethereum (EVM) and Solana (non-EVM) chains
   - Immediate USDC-to-USD off-ramp via Circle
   - Direct transfer to DriveWealth brokerage account for instant trading
   - Reverse flow for withdrawals (USD → USDC)

3. **Investment Capabilities**
   - Curated investment baskets (5-10 expert-designed portfolios)
   - Stock and ETF trading
   - Options trading
   - Simple portfolio view with real-time performance tracking

4. **AI CFO (MVP Version)**
   - Automated weekly performance summaries
   - On-demand portfolio analysis highlighting diversification and risk
   - Built in Go, integrates with DriveWealth data

5. **Brokerage Integration**
   - Secure backend integration with DriveWealth for trade execution
   - Custody of traditional assets through partner infrastructure

### Post-MVP Vision
- Advanced conversational AI CFO with proactive nudges
- Social features: profiles, following, leaderboards, copy investing
- User-curated baskets and community-driven portfolios
- Debit card, P2P payments, time-lock investments
- Business accounts and startup launchpad

## Use Cases

### Primary Use Cases
1. **First-Time Investor:** User signs up, deposits USDC, invests in a curated "Tech Growth" basket within minutes
2. **Crypto-to-Stocks Bridge:** User converts crypto holdings to USDC, seamlessly invests in traditional assets
3. **Portfolio Monitoring:** User checks AI CFO weekly summaries to understand performance and risk
4. **Quick Withdrawal:** User sells positions, receives USDC back to their preferred blockchain

### Value Delivery
- **Speed:** Instant funding and trading vs. 3-5 day ACH transfers
- **Simplicity:** No seed phrases, no gas fee calculations, no complex wallet management
- **Trust:** AI CFO protection, curated baskets, transparent fee structure
- **Accessibility:** Mobile-first design matching Gen Z digital expectations

## Business Goals
- **Adoption:** 10,000 Monthly Active Users (MAU) within 6 months of launch
- **Monetization:** 5% conversion from free to premium tier in Year 1
- **Validation:** $1,000,000 in processed investment volume in Year 1
- **Positioning:** First mover in Gen Z-native hybrid Web3 + traditional finance space

## Success Metrics
- **Engagement:** Daily/Monthly Active Users, session frequency
- **Retention:** Week 1, Month 1, Month 3 retention rates
- **Conversion:** Sign-up → Funded Account rate; Free → Premium rate
- **Financial:** Total Assets Under Management (AUM)
- **User Sentiment:** NPS scores, confidence surveys, safety perception
